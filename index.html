International Conference of Young Algebraists in Thailand (ICYAT)

About the Conference

The Conference of Young Algebraists in Thailand (ICYAT) is a prominent academic event dedicated to the field of algebra and related areas. Established in 1996 by Tiang Poomsa-ard and his colleagues at Khon Kaen University, the conference began under the name Conference of Young Algebraists in Thailand (CYAT) as a platform for mathematicians across Thailand to share research findings, exchange ideas, and foster collaborations.

Since its inception, the conference has been held annually in November and hosted by various institutions throughout Thailand, including:

  • Chiang Mai University
  • Mahasarakham University
  • Maejo University
  • Rajamangala University of Technology Isan (Khon Kaen Campus)
  • Silpakorn University (Sanam Chan Palace Campus)
  • Udon Thani Rajabhat University

In 2019, the 15th CYAT—held at Chiang Mai University—marked the conference's first international edition. Following this milestone, the event was renamed the Conference of Young Algebraists in Thailand (ICYAT).

ICYAT now serves as a venue for students, faculty members, and researchers working in algebra, lattice theory, and related topics to present their work, engage in academic dialogue, and build meaningful collaborations. The conference continues to promote the advancement of algebraic research both within Thailand and internationally.
